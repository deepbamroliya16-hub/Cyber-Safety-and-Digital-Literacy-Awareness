Core Principle

Three Simple Rules

✋
STOP

Pause and think before clicking or sharing anything online.

🔍
CHECK

Verify senders and information through trusted official sources.

🛡️
PROTECT

Secure accounts, update devices, and seek help when something seems wrong.
