Module 5 of 6

Be a Responsible
Digital Citizen

Your digital footprint lasts — make it responsible and constructive. How you use technology today shapes your opportunities tomorrow.

The Three Pillars

Respect. Verify. Contribute.

🤝
01 — Respect Others

Communicate politely, avoid hurtful posts, and do not share private information about others online. Treat people the same way you would face-to-face.

🔍
02 — Verify Before Sharing

Check sources before passing on information. Trust reputable outlets and use fact-check tools. Spreading misinformation — even unintentionally — causes real harm.

🚀
03 — Use Tech Positively

Leverage online tools for learning, creativity, and community — while following rules and digital ethics. Technology is a tool; how you use it is your choice.

Daily Habits

Quick Cyber Safety Tips

🔐

Secure Accounts

Use strong passwords and enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on every important account.

🔄

Maintain Devices

Update apps and operating systems regularly to patch known security vulnerabilities.

🧠

Think Before Sharing

Verify information and avoid posting sensitive personal data publicly or in unknown groups.

🚪

Log Out Always

Always log out from shared or public devices, and clear browsing history when done.

Conclusion

3 Simple Rules to Live By

✋
STOP

Pause and think before clicking or sharing anything online.

🔍
CHECK

Verify senders and information through trusted official sources.

🛡️
PROTECT

Secure accounts, update devices, and seek help when something seems wrong.

"Your digital footprint lasts — make it responsible and constructive."
